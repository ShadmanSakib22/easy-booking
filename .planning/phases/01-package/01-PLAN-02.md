<objective>
Implement the fixed date-range feature (D-04 through D-07). Add optional `startDate` and `endDate` props to `<Calendar>`, thread them through `CalendarContext`, initialize the view to `startDate`'s month on mount, disable (not hide) the Toolbar prev/next buttons at the range boundaries, and grey out plus disable interaction on grid dates outside the range.

Purpose: A SaaS booking calendar is bounded — a creator publishes availability for a specific window (e.g. May 19–25). Visitors must not navigate to or click empty months outside that window. This is a pure additive feature: omitting the props preserves today's unbounded behavior.
Output: Date-range-aware Calendar with constrained navigation and greyed out-of-range cells; green dateRange tests from Plan 00.
</objective>
